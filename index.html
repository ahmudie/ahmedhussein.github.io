God of
Cardboard

Gods of Cardboard is rouguelike game. the inspiration came from Paper Mario, God of War and the Binding of Isaac. You play as Kratos or Hermes. You heard about rumors that the almighty hydra managed to get in the Olympus. You need to make your way into the Olympus and fight against the Hydra. the game was built with the Monogame Engine and C#.

Archeology

VR-Archeology is like the name said an Archeology-Simulator in VR. Our goal is to bring the player more information about Archeology near to him. With a team about 8 people we maked with help of Unity and Blender a Beta-Game about how to manage a museum, dig a skeleton or fossils and processing them for the museum. this game is still work in progress

Balls
Invasions

Balls Invasions is a jump and run high score game with the inspiration from space invaders. You play as an Alien with a passion to defend his homeplanet from the monstreus balls and it have some basics game-elements like an highscore system.
