Stronks

Stronks is 3rd-Person top down gathering Game. Your goal is ton collect all members of your people to get together stong and fight against mammoths. Then you need to collect food to get health back after a fight. You can collect members and change your formation of the team for the best attack. This Project was created from 3 People and was builded with Unity.
